APES Final Project

=== Fishing and Mining Game ===

Start with money (only can be seen in bank)
- You have enough money to start either a mining or fishing company
Buy companies from Fishing or Mining buildings
Manage company(ies) from Company building
 - Can advance by months (up to a year)
 - Earn money based on how well company(ies) do in that time
In Fishing or Mining buildings, can upgrade different things from upgrade tree
 - Can upgrade with money over time
Fishing
 - Drift-netting
 - Trawling
 - Long-lining
Mining
 - Mountaintop removal
 - Strip
 - Sub-surface
 - Open pit
 - Placer

Fonts:
 - System bold 10pt (for tree)
Upgrade Paths (trees):
 - image 0:
   - Basic color 3x3
   - Black Text 
   - Black Outline
 - image 1:
   - Basic color 3x3 w/ black 145 opacity fill
 - image 2:
   - Basic color 3x3 w/ white 145 opacity fill
